About Us

Our approach

Two hands massage therapy provides treatment specific to your individual needs, whether you are looking to manage an injury, reduce pain or take some time to de-stress and unwind.

Self-care is difficult to make time for and justify spending money on which is why our services are reasonably priced. If you are unable to pay the full amount, a Koha payment option is available which can be arranged when you book.

We offer deep tissue massage and relaxation massage at our Christchurch and Lyttleton locations for 45, 60 or 90 minute sessions, book now so we can get you feeling good, moving well and living your best life!

How we can help you

Massage therapy can help take you out of the busy thinking mind and into the feeling body. Sometimes the most impactful thing you can do to be productive is to rest and reset. It’s important to remember that you are a human being not a human doing, so give yourself a break from the doing and nourish your body mind and soul with Two hands massage therapy. Massage can help with a wide variety of conditions including; injury rehabilitation, chronic pain disorders, headaches/ migraines, anxiety, depression and insomnia.

Who we are

Kia ora! My name is Saskia, I am a massage therapist, yoga teacher and I am currently studying to become a registered Osteopath. I offer relaxation, therapeutic and deep tissue massage catering to your individual needs. I am a firm believer that the body, under the right conditions has the innate capacity to heal itself and I am here to help facilitate that healing.

Training:
  • Bachelor of Musculoskeletal health – Ara Institute of Canterbury 2023 leading into graduate diploma in Osteopathy 2024
  • Certificate in Fitness- Ara Institute of Canterbury, 2018
  • 200hr RYA YTT, Wild Self Yoga, 2018
  • Holistic Massage Therapy Level 3, Holistic Health Training 2023
  • Deep Tissue Massage Therapy Level 3, Holistic Health Training 2023

For me, massage has always seemed like a very indulgent thing to spend money on but through my own healing I have come to respect the importance of partaking in practices that encourage self love. I feel that massage is a self discovery process which allows an individual to tune into what different parts of the body is holding onto physically, emotionally and energetically.

My intention with massage is to promote softness, stillness and space, clearing physical tensions to allow for mental clarity and free flowing energy. I want to help people feel comfortable and empowered in their own body, allowing vulnerability and connection to the people and world around them.
